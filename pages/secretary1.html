SECRETARY

SMT. USHA GANGADHARA

secretary , sdc group of institutions

Chairman

Sri. Gangadhar Chairman of SDC Educational Trust, Kolar, is the son of Smt.Danamma and Sri Channabasavaiah. Sri Gangadhar is a business man by profession and a great visionary and Philanthropist. The act of charity is in his genes. Sri. Gangadhar's grand father Sri.Chikka Chennajappa Chetty built Administrative block at Kamala Nehru Sanitorium and donated for the public. He also knew that the people of Kolar are suffering for water so he built a water tank (Opp to KEB Office) . This great Philanthropist did not stop there, he has constructed a hospital for the coffee estate workers of Chickamagaluru district at Malapurahalli Village. Thus hospital is named after this great personality known as "Kolar Chikka Chennajappa Chetty combined Hospital". The Maharaja of Mysore has awarded him with "LOKSEVA NIRATHA" and "DHARMA PRAKASHA' These awards were bestowed for his long selfless service for the poor irrespective of caste and creed. Sri. Gangadhar hails from this great family had a vision to provide Education to all sections of the society at a very affordable cost, with the quality of high standards available at other places for the rural and down trodden people of Kolar District. Smt. Usha Gangadhar Secretary of the trust is a charimatic personality, a dynamic and stately leader. She is another pillar for the trust. This couple has laid a strong platform for this educational institution. Within the short span of time, SDC College has proved the best college in Kolar District. This couple think that the best act of charity to any human being is giving them education, because education enables humans to become civilized , i t enriches their caliber , uplifts the life through economy and the most important it makes to become the respectable human beings
