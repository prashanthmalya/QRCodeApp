Smt. Danamma Channabasavaiah College of Arts, Commerce, Science and Management Studies

Smt Danamma Channabasavaiah College of Arts Commerce and Science & Management Studies is situated in Kolar in Karnataka state of India. Established in 2008, it is accredited from NAAC and it is affiliated to Bangalore University. SDCCACSM, Kolar offers 5 courses across 4 streams namely Commerce and Banking, Management, IT, Science and across 4 degrees like BSc, BCA, BBA, B.Com.Hostel facility is not available for its students. Additional campus facilities such as Math Lab, Canteen, Computer Lab, Library, Medical Facilities, Cls. Room are also there.
